🌀 Electric Motor Control

I work on fault-tolerant control strategies for electric motors used in aeronautical propulsion, focusing on robustness, reliability, and efficiency. This includes Model-Based Design in MATLAB/Simulink and real-time testing with Speedgoat platforms.

🔋 Battery Management Systems

I develop observer-based algorithms for estimating SOC and SOH in Li-ion batteries. These algorithms are implemented and validated on embedded platforms for aviation-grade reliability.
